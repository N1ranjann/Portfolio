01 — Overview

Built for the
physical world.

Mosaic is an elite, production-grade retail analytics platform designed to transform physical store telemetry into high-precision, actionable business intelligence.

Unlike invasive optical camera-based tracking systems, Mosaic leverages high-frequency Ultra-Wideband (UWB) radio telemetry to track anonymous customer trajectories at centimeter-level accuracy — fully safeguarding shopper privacy.

"Physical retail deserved the same intelligence layer that e-commerce has had for decades."

The result: dwell-time heatmaps, footfall patterns, zone conversion metrics, and real-time crowd density — delivered without capturing a single frame of video.

02 — The Problem

The surveillance
paradox.

Modern retailers face a paradox: e-commerce platforms have granular session-level data on every click and scroll, while physical stores operate largely blind — relying on manual counts and gut instinct.

Existing solutions almost universally use optical cameras with facial recognition or silhouette tracking. These carry enormous privacy risks, erode customer trust, and face growing legal restrictions across the globe.

Mosaic closes this intelligence gap without crossing the privacy line. UWB radio signals can track movement to within a few centimeters, operating entirely in the abstract — no image, no identity, no risk.

What Mosaic
delivers.

    UWB Trajectory Tracking — Centimeter-precision anonymous path mapping across the entire store floor, without any optical input.
